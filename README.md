# BEwebinar

- Course materials for Webinar *Time Series: A First Course with Bootstrap Starter*.
- A shortened version of the course was given by Tucker McElroy and Dimitris Politis in April 2021.
- The current version of the course, in 12 chapters and 84 lessons, was sponsored by the Business and Economics section of the ASA, with webinars given in spring and summer of 2025.
- This same material is planned for a series of webinars sponsored by NISS, for spring and summer of 2026.
- Included are Rmd and html renderings for the 84 lessons, as well as data sets needed for the analyses.
